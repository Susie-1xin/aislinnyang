How I got here

I grew up in a small city in Jiangxi and did the very normal student route for eighteen years: school, exams, repeat. No cinematic prodigy arc, sadly. The main plot was that I was nosy in a useful way. I wanted to know how successful people thought, what other lives looked like, and what existed outside the tiny map I had been given.

Then I moved to Hangzhou for university and the map got bigger very quickly. During sophomore year I found RW Lab by accident, which introduced me to Web3, digital nomads, startups, and people building things just because they could. Up to that point, most of my learning came from syllabuses. RW was the first place where learning felt like wandering into a room I actually wanted to stay in.
